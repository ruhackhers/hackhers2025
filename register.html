HackHERS is Rutgers University's annual, women-centric 24-hour hackathon. We strive to create a space in which women** can come together and be empowered to build innovative and creative projects, and we welcome all genders to partake in this mission.

Website: https://ruhackhers.org/
Dates: February 8th-9th, 2025
Location: College Avenue Student Center (126 College Ave, New Brunswick, NJ 08901)

Note: You have to be residing in the US and attending an US institution in order to attend HackHERS.

*FYI - Doors open at 11am on Saturday and close at 5pm on Sunday. The space will be open overnight on Saturday for hackers to continue hacking. Lunch and dinner will be provided on Saturday and breakfast and lunch will be provided on Sunday :)

**We use the inclusive definition of "women" and "female" and we welcome trans women, genderqueer women, and non-binary people.
